Popular Destinations

1 Sky Diving For the ultimate thrill, skydive over the stunning Palm Jumeirah or the Arabian Desert. As you freefall from 13,000 feet, you’ll be treated to breathtaking aerial views of Dubai’s skyline, coastline, and desert landscape. Jump To Euphoria
2 Dune Bashing Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush as you speed across the golden sands of the desert in a 4x4 vehicle. Dune bashing is a must-try activity in Dubai’s deserts, where you’ll navigate steep dunes, sharp turns, and thrilling drops. Ride on Sands
3 Hot Air Balloon Rides For a more serene adventure, take a hot air balloon ride over the desert at sunrise. Soaring over the vast sands, you’ll catch breathtaking views of the desert landscape and spot wildlife like Arabian oryx and gazelles. Look from Top
4 Indoor Skiing At Ski Dubai in the Mall of the Emirates, you can hit the slopes even in the middle of the desert! This indoor ski resort features ski runs, snowboarding areas, and even an area to play with real penguins. Just Snowing
5 Jet Skiing Explore Dubai’s stunning coastline on a jet ski. Ride across the waters of the Arabian Gulf while taking in views of the Burj Al Arab and Dubai Marina’s impressive skyline. Bash the Waters
6 Zip Lining at XLine Experience the world’s longest urban zip line at XLine Dubai Marina, which stretches over 1 kilometer. As you zip through the air at speeds of up to 80 km/h, you’ll get a bird’s-eye view of the Marina, skyscrapers, and yachts below. Fly in Miles
7 Scuba Diving Dive into the underwater world of the Arabian Gulf and explore vibrant coral reefs, shipwrecks, and marine life. Scuba diving in Dubai is a great way to discover the region’s rich aquatic ecosystem. Swim to depths
